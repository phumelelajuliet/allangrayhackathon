Read and Release

From unwanted christmas presents to books bought on a whim, even the most avid of readers find themselves with treasure troves of books they have not touched in a while and probably never will. As with much in life it's sometimes necessary to let go of the old so as to make way for the new. These amazing places are scattered all over and some can even come to you to collect your beloved stash.

Wisdom of the Crowd

With the advent of technology, it only makes sense to make use of this 21st century magic to share and discover the magic found within books. The Book Odyssey mines for the latest trends and patterns in the bookworm to find new and old gems. We then reach out to our network of trusty pre-loved charity bookstores to find the titles in question.

Building Book Repositories

Young South African's from disadvantaged neighborhoods are being left behind in terms of reading abilities in comparison to their more affluent counterparts. This is especially evident in the reading abilities of students in primary schools. There are a number of organisation now working to tackle the problem. Assistance is needed in getting books for the establishment of local book repositories within communities. A donation of $5 goes towards the purchase of a book from the pre-loved charity bookstores

Caffeinated Curation

Amazing books were made for big mugs. Using our amazing machine learning genie we are able to assist coffee shops in acquiring books for their chillspots. Good books means your clientele stays for longer and we share and encourage the love of books.

Future Fantasies

We believe that finances should not inhibit the growth of the reading community. The secondhand book community helps readers access books at affordable prices. However, it is taxing trying to track down specific titles from one far flung charity bookstore to the next. That's where we at The Book Odyssey are excited to offer ways to make this experience less taxing by sourcing the books for you. You can collect them after we have located them or we can have them delivered to you. Note down your details and you will be the first to know when the service is available.
